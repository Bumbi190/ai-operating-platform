# The Prompt — Brand Identity

## Name
**The Prompt**
- Handle: @theprompt
- Tagline (working): *"AI news, delivered daily."*

## Concept
An AI-native news channel covering artificial intelligence developments for a global English-speaking audience. Fast, factual, premium — Bloomberg QuickTake meets the AI era.

- Trustworthy, not hype
- Fast-paced, punchy sentences
- Smart but accessible — no jargon without explanation
- Narrator: Victoria (ElevenLabs) — warm, authoritative
